Domain Square Important Update

The PC Gaming Event for Domain Square postponed till further notice due to the number of registrations going through the roof and very little time available to hold the event with the summer break approaching.

However, because of the huge demand for console gaming, we have decided to have a console only(PS3 and XBox 360) gaming event in place of the PC Gaming event on the 12th and 13th of May, 2010. The event will be held after school hours. Please note that the Console Gaming event is NOT related to the PC Gaming event in anyway,

Rules

  • Only the first 100 registrations for the Console Event will be entertained. All current registrations for PC Gaming events as of 6th may are null and void.
  • An email from your either of your parents or guardian permitting you to stay back and participate in the event must reach exunclan+d2@gmail.com by the 11th of May. Registrations without parental permission will not be entertained.
  • You may not choose which console you wish to play on. You’ll be randomly assigned a console and opponent on the spot for a knockout match.
  • Games will not be disclosed beforehand so please don’t bother asking.
  • The event will be held after school hours. You must arrange for your own transport.
