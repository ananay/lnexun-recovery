Creating the next version of Kindle for Amazon

Problem statement for Exun’s Creative Event Finals

Congratulations on making it to the final round of the e-Lite 2017 Creative Event! We’re thrilled to see what you can make within the given time frame of 1.5 hours and an internet connection.

Introduction

With a thousand books packed into a small device – Amazon Kindle — The portable ebook reader has been making waves in the industry. Amazon is still doing well with the sales, but it feels that they’ve overlooked something; which is why they want to step up their game.

The Task

Your task is to conceptualize and create the next version of Kindle. They want your ideas and creativity to act as valuable inputs so as to keep up their success in the market.

Design

Your task is to design a neat User Interface for the Kindle and give it’s users a flawless User Experience.

Web Development

Your task is to make a landing page describing the new features of the Kindle. Bonus Points shall be awarded if you make a functional UI of the Kindle.

You’re allowed to use the internet for reference, but you cannot use templates.

3D Modelling

Your task is to design the 3D form factor of the new Kindle.

Audio

Your task is to make an audio track for Kindle’s current advertisement.

Good Luck!
