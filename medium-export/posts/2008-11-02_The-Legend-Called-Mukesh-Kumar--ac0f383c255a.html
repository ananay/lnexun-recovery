The Legend Called Mukesh Kumar

Ladies and Gentlemen, Today, 3rd November is Mukesh Sir’s Birthday. I would like to take this opportunity to write a few lines on what Mukesh Sir means to The Exun Clan.

Exun stands where it is today, only because of Mukesh Sir’s guidance and never ending trust. Exun is not a computer club. It is a family held together by Mukesh Sir’s leadership, love and affection. Without him, Exun was just another ‘club’. Thats why its called the Exun Clan. We not I.

Here is wishing Mukesh Sir a happy birthday from all Exunites and many more happy years ahead.
