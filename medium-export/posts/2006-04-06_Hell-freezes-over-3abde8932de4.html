Mac do windows too…

yes , it true . apple now gives users an official way to boot XP on your mac.
Apple has released “bootcamp “ that lets you install and run the Windows XP operating system on your Mac . its available as a public beta here .

Bootcamp creates CD with all drivers for the hardware and a new partition onto which you can then load windows ( using your own copy ) and you can choose between the two during start up . Just make sure you have an intel mac and enough space on your drive .

apple says this technology will be a feature in its next edition of mac OSX( leopard).
