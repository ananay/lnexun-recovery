The Class VII and VIII logic Test.

Good day.



It has been a fair bit of time since I have written, but now I have something to report, so I will report it.

The test was a major success, and we actually needed another lab to fit in all the people that showed up. Class VII appeared to have assembled in great big marshaled companies, and we had to get the papers photocopied twice, as our original expected number was way below the actual attendance. The actual answer-scripts have not been corrected as of now and are currently lying in Mr. Mukesh’s room, which I will take the opportunity to dub “The Vault of Exun”. The results will probably be out on Wednesday.



I expect a good performance from the seventhies, better than the one given by Class VIII students, though admittedly the paper was a bit easier this time.



 — Nisheeth

By Ananay Arora on September 6, 2004.
