General Rules:

1. It should be made sure that participants DO NOT register for events that may possibly clash.

2. The schools must send one of their representatives during the registration time each day to register for the events of that day.

3. Judges’ decisions are final in all events. Rules are subject to change. Please visit our site for any such changes.

Please Note:

Software/Installation/Presentation CD’s (no floppy please) for events Software Display and Multimedia Presentation, must be submitted on the First Day i.e. on Thursday, 21st December 2006, at the time of Registration along with well-formatted documentation and/or source-code.

You can download the event details, schedule, other important details, etc. from their school website http://www.modernschool.net/access/access2006.zip
