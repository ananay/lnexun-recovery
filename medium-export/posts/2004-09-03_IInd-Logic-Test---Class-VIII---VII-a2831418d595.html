IInd Logic Test — Class VIII & VII

Another logic test will be conducted for students of class VIII and class VII for exun programming entrance on 6th September 2004 (Monday) during stay back hours. Students who want to give the test may or maynot be familiar to Basic/C++ programming language. Students will be tested only on their logical and analytical skills (only simple mathematical logic required). They wouldnot be asked to write any programs. Posters have already been sent to each section and have been fixed on school notice boards. Hopefully there will be a better response than last time.

By Ananay Arora on September 3, 2004.
