D.P.S R.K.Puram 40 Years Journey

One of the most prestigious schools in India and the home of the ExunClan, Delhi Public School, R.K.Puram, celebrated 40 years of ‘Excellence in Education’ (1972–2012) on Wednesday, 18th April, 2012.

Her Excellency, Smt. Pratibha Devisingh Patil, Hon’ble President of India was the Chief Guest on the occasion.

The celebrations saw a series of cultural performances including Orchestral and Dance Performances by Students of D.P.S.R.K.Puram as well as junior students of D.P.S.Vasant Vihar and D.P.S.
