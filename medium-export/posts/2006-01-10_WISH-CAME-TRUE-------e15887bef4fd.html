Presenting new iMac and MacBook Pro with INTEL DUAL CORE!!!

Apple has unveiled new iMac and Upgrade to PowerBook.. i.e. MacBook Pro with Dual Core Intel Processor. The new iMac is upto 2 times faster and macbook pro delivers upto 4 times horsepower as powerbook G4.

MacBook Pro also comes with Front Row (with apple remote) and iSight Camera. It is currently available in two models i.e. 1.67 Ghz and 1.83Ghz. The size for both models is 15.4inch. Graphics Memory is increased to 256MB in 1.83Ghz model. Rest everything is same. The 1.83 Ghz model cost same as 17inch Powerbook.

And don’t worry about applications. Rosetta is built into Mac OS X..(Intel one). However Pro apps like Final Cut etc.. will need to be upgraded. Also iLife 06 and iWork 06 are out.
