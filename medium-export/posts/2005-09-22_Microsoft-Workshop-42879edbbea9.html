Microsoft Workshop

Microsoft Workshop for students of Exun was organized on 22nd September 2005 in the 5th-6th Periods. Mr. Aswani Sharma ( email: ashwanis@microsoft.com ), Academic Developer Evangelist, exposed students to the Latest programming environments. Nine students from class XI & XII attended the same. The workshop was very interesting and students were enthusiastic to know more and more happenings in the field of programming environments.
