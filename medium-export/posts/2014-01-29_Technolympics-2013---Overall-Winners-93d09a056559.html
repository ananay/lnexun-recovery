Technolympics 2013 — Overall Winners

Technolympics 2013 was held at Cambridge School, Noida on 28th January 2014. Exun Clan bagged the Overall Winners Trophy. The individual results are as follows:-

  • Shagun Goel
  • Parth Mittal
  • Moksh Talreja [NE]
  • Ananay Arora
  • Shashwat Goel
  • Aman Verma
  • Saumitra Khullar
  • Shiven Mian
  • Anshuman Dixit
  • Sudhanshu Agarwal

Congratulations to all the Winners!
