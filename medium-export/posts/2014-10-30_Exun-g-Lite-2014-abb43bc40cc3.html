Exun g-Lite 2014

The Exun Clan presents Exun g-Lite 2014, Exun’s annual recruitment event open to all girls from classes 6th to 11th. The event will be divided into two categories :

  • Online Event : Creative Event
     The Creative Event entails the following sub-events :
  • Web Design
  • Digital Imaging
  • Audio-Video
  • Onsite Events

The Onsite Events will be held on 10th November, 2014. and will comprise of a myriad of events all connected to the diverse and ever changing world of technology. Further details are availablehere.
