Domain Square 2010

Exun Clan presents Domain Square, the ultimate intraschool battleground for gaming.

The Event will be held on 5th & 6th August after school hours. To register, follow the link below:

Domain Square 2010 Registration

Rules

  • You may participate in more than one event, provided you register beforehand.
  • NO headphones/earphones will be provided. You must bring your own. You’re also allowed to carry a USB Mouse.
  • The event is open to students of classes 9th to 12th of DPS R.K. Puram only.
  • If your clan members are absent, tough luck. You CANNOT add new members on the spot. You’ll have to play with whichever members of your team turned up.
  • The event will be after school hours. You will have to arrange your own transport.
