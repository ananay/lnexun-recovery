Yahoo! 360 Invites available

If you need a Yahoo! 360 invitation, please leave a comment. You are sure to receive one.

It’s just another blogging and photo sharing tool from Yahoo!

You have to keep one thing in mind — due to some inexplicable policy, you have to be above 18 to use this service. So, when you sign up, choose an appropriate date for your DoB or change your current settings.

PS — such invites are not easy to find, so please don’t ask for one for the heck of it.
