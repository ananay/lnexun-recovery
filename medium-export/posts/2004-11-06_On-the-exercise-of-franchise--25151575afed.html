On the exercise of franchise.

Most people will argue that this entry was better posted as a comment, but that would undermine its importance and that is a risk that I cannot take. In a recent MSN chat with Karan, I have been advised to ask all members to vote for or against the idea of putting a Firefox button on the lnexun home page. In the previous post, this idea was opposed by none other than our sleeping president, Maanick Nangia, on grounds that because Mozilla does not sponsor us we should not have their logo on the homepage.

In my opinion this entire line of thinking is inherently defunct. We are not a corporate entity, we do not have to do everything on a profit only basis. We can and should do things because they are the right thing to do. Remember itÂ’s Exun, not Exun Inc.

Putting a button-link on the front page can hurt no one, but it might do good to a lot of people still stuck on Internet Explorer. Helping the spread of Firefox is just a good old fashioned right thing to do.

I suggested leave your votes as comments. Ayes support, Nays oppose.

— Nisheeth.
