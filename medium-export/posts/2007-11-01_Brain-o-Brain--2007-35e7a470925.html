Brain-o Brain -2007

Brain-o Brain -2007

Day : 6th & 7th November 2007

Venue : Chinmaya Vidyalaya, Vasant Vihar, New Delhi–57.
 Last date for Registration : 1st Nov. 2007.

Time of Registration on

6th & 7th November 2007 : 8.00 a.m. EVENTS

1. Web Designing (6th November 2007)
 No. of Participants:-2
 Eligibility: VIII & IX

  1. Topic will be given during the time of competition
  2. Clips will be provided
  3. Students will create a website on the topic given in HTML
  4. Photoshop 6.5 will be provided

2. Group Discussion (6th November 2007)
 No. of Participant:-1
 Eligibility: IX to XII

  1. One speaker from each school
  2. Topic and Rules will be announced on the spot
  3. 5 teams will qualify from the first round for the final round

3. MS. Paint (6th November 2007)
 No. of Participants:-2
 Eligibility: IV & V

  1. Topic will be given during the time of competition
  2. Clips will be provided

4. Quiz (6th & 7th November 2007)
 No. of Participants:-2
 Eligibility: IX above

  1. Quiz based on computers and technology
  2. 6 teams will qualify from the written preliminary round for the final on stage round

5. Gaming (6th & 7th November 2007)
 Eligibility: Open for any class

  1. One participant from each school
  2. Rules will be announced on the spot

6. Programming in “C++” or “Visual Basic” (7th November 2007)
 No. of Participants:-2
 Eligibility: XI & XII

  1. Two Questions to be solved either in “C++” or “VB”
  2. Software:-Turbo C++ or Visual Basic 6.0.

7. Presentation in Power Point (7th November 2007)
 No. of Participants:-2
 Eligibility: VI & VII

  1. Topic will be given during the time of competition
  2. Clips will be provided

8. Movie in Flash (7th November 2007)
 No. of Participants:-2
 Eligibility: VIII above

  1. Topic will be given during the time of competition
  2. Movie should not be of more than 1minutes otherwise student will be disqualified.

NOTE:
Participants should make sure that they do not participate in events that are occurring at the same time. If time left surprise event will also be organized.
