Ordinatrix 2004 at Tagore International School (29th July)

There is a two-event competition on 29th July.



It has Web Designing (Classes 6 to 9) and Movie Making (Classes 10 to 12). The Movie Making is weird, and I don’t know how it works, but for Web Designing, you need to make a picture based on one of the topics they’ve given beforehand and bring it on a CD.



Topics for Web Designing

  • Supermarket
  • Aeronautics
  • Fashion Designing
  • News
  • Travel Agency

They have a website http://www.tagoreint.com/
