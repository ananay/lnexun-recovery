Nano with a new touch!

Apple’s iPod Nano really has come a long way since its inception in 2o05. With the sixth generation in tow, Apple kept it slim and lithe, but to really keep true to its essence, the new iPod Nano is tinier. Yes, it is as small as the current version of the iPod Shuffle, and its sports a multi-touch screen as well.

The new iPod Nano, as usual, features a lot of vibrant colors, and you can clip it to your favorite shirt or the pocket of your trousers. Talk about hands-free music! Storage is presently available at 8GB or 16GB.

Check out the new Nano — http://www.apple.com/ipodnano/
