The Exun e-Lite 2015 Senior Quiz Finals were held on 10 July 2015, and the winners are as follows:

  1. Aryaman Dubey (XI-J) and Aditya Sengupta (XI-I)
  2. Srijan Jain (XI-K) and Parth Rangan (XI-C)
  3. Aveneel Waadhwa (XI-L) and Dev Shaurya Singhal (XI-O)

Congratulations to the winners! All position holders will be interviewed before induction.
